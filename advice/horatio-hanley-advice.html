Hi, my name is Horatio and I have some advice for you!

Determination!

Whenever you feel stuck and feel like giving up, remember why you started in the first place. Nothing worth it is ever easy and if it's easy ask yourself "Is it really worth it ?"

Consistency.

To become proficient at any skills repititon and routine is neccessary. Take walking for instance, when you were younger looking at the world around being mobile, you started to strive to walk. But as everyone learning something new you fell or hit an obstacle and what did you do ? You took a breather and tried again.

Patience!

This is crucial in the learning process but only you can guage what this means for you. This is your journey....
