Hi, my name is Jen and I have some advice!

Be prepared for frustration!

There will be a point when you hit a wall and feel like this.

Ignore it and keep working. Ask questions. Google search. Use Slack. Be persistent, curious, and determined.

You are not that, you are this.

Even when you forget or ignore it.

Use other resources

If you're not connecting the dots or getting the practice you need for the info to stick, try free coding courses elsewhere.

Sometimes learning with another teacher or viewpoint can change how you put 2 + 2 together.

Take a break

Step away and do something else for a few days. Try a favorite hobby!

Coding can be like trying to remember a forgotten piece of info, only to remember it hours later. It's good to re-charge and get a little perspective.

When all else fails, cats!
