Progress NOT Perfection

  • Just because you don't immediately understand a brand new concept doesn't mean it isn't worthwhile undergoing the learning process!
  • The concepts are building blocks to each other; pace yourself while tackling challenges.
  • Mistakes = golden opportunities to learn!
  • Reach out! Your techie peers are just as equally eager to learn from each other as they are teaching each other!
  • Instructors are there to help you along the way - don't be shy in requesting and digesting their feedback!
  • Practice, practice, PRACTICE, so you can build confidence in your newfound skills!
