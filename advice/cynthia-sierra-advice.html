Hi, my name is Cynthia and I have some advice for you!

Review the lessons again. And again, and again!

I wanted to get everything right the first time, and I wanted to see that green check at the end of each lesson upon completion! I was so confident until I got to the projects. I felt confused, scared and stressed. This is not for me, I thought.

Then I remembered a saying we have in Spanish..."es una carrera, no son carreritas" which translates to "it's a career, not a race"..which, actually does not work in english but, you get the idea. Just reviewing the lessons and taking some notes was a complete changer for me. Writing just basic things down made them really stick and helped me remember everything better.

Take a break!

I was very keen on finishing the lessons before taking breaks, and realized sometimes I was making very silly mistakes. It's useful to go out, take a walk, have some tea, or come back tomorrow. A clear mind and fresh eyes will help more than hours of staring at your computer and try to figure out where you went wrong.
