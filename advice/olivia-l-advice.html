Practice Makes Perfect!

When you first start your journey into the tech world, it can feel super overwhelming and challenging.

Some questions you may have asked yourself:

  • How can I code a dope yet functional website from scratch?
  • Will I be really able to switch careers into tech?
  • What if it gets too difficult for me and I feel like giving up?

Remember, we all have to start somewhere. No one becomes a professional web developer or fabulous visual designer overnight. The hardest part is simply to start!

Make a schedule that works for you, learn one lesson at a time, and take the time to practice to find what works and what doesn't. Creating these small habits can end up being super rewarding in the end.

Take Advantage of the Awesome Skillcrush Community.

One thing I love about Skillcrush is that there is no such thing as a dumb question. So don't hesitate to reach out to your instructors or other fellow Skillcrushers on Slack and ask away whenever you're feeling stuck.

Everyone is super supportive and learns from one another. It might just be my favorite part of joining Skillcrush.
