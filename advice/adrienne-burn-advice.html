Confusion is your friend!

If you are a beginner at web development, understand that consistency and pacients are the two key factors of of not only getting better overtime but having a future skill that you can use in almost any company you want to work for and or for yourself. There will always be an obstacle or a challenge but never give up.

It is very important to ask questions or do research during the process of web developing. Do not put too much pressure on yourself, remember practice makes perfection; and in do time it will become easier to understand how different softwares are used to create an excellent website. It is important to network, find websites that you feel helps you to better understand, take notes, practice a lot, and never be afraid to ask questions.

Suspended disbelief plays an important role.

When you are learning a new technology, whether you are learning to write your first programming language, or just the latest framework, you go through this uncomfortable phase where you know that the code you are looking at is doing this THING but you don't understand HOW exactly it's happening.

This is a normal part of the process of learning a new technology, and in fact, it's a critical part of the process.

You see when you encounter a new programming language part of your learning process includes learning to train your eye. The way to train your eye is to read a good deal of code even when you don't totally understand what it's doing.
