Hi, my name is Otter Woman and I have some counsel for you!

Go easy on yourself … but not TOO easy

Yes, learning to code is hard. But if you constantly tell yourself this, it can lead to giving yourself a break. And you need to keep at it! Of course, get up and walk around the block, stretch, play with your fuzzy companion, but then get back in front of that computer, focus, and bend your mind to this stuff. It'll get easier with consistent thinking and practicing.

Drink water, get plenty of good rest, and stock your fridge with healthy foods

Brain fog definitely won't help you learn programming faster. Neither will being tired or hungry. Don't forget to take care of yourself so your machine functions are all running smoothly.

Have fun!

If you're getting into this in an attempt to pivot careers or gain the know-how to establish a lucrative side hustle, don't look at it as the work you need to do to get the work. Try thinking of it as a challenging game that will give you insight to greater satisfaction. And after putting some solid time in, do what really makes you happy, tick, feel alive, be you.
