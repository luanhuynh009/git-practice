Hi, my name is Doina and I have some advice for you!

Make it happen

When you start programming your first web app you are doing something that you have never done before. It's a new challenge, a new opportunity, and in the case of software development, something so specific that it's probably never been done before.

Even though everything is challenging for you right now, be sure you can do this. Just take your time and be patient.

Practice every day

While you're learning this new stuff practice what you've learned in order to stick with it.

Also, I would like to highlight that you should make a habit. That means to block out a specific time when you know you're productive and practice every day.

Having a growth mindset will help you to overcome your uncertainty. You've got this!
