Never Give Up!

If you are confused then that means you are exploring an area of knowledge yet to be put together like a puzzle. The brain will make excuses to stop doing the work to better your future because it wants the short term feel good vibes. The brain has been made to help your survive and change is a scary thing so when something new arrives it will do what it can to distract and to convince you that there is no need to learn new material. So for the sake of your future self while the mind may make sweet whispers in your ear to watch Netflix or check your social media, remember that you are changing your future for the better.
