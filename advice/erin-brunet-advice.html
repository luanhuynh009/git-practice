Hi, my name is Erin and I have some advice for you!

You Got This!

You took the leap to try something new. It's a new challenge, a new opportunity, and you have to have faith in your self and your new skills.

Don't be afraid to make mistakes. We cannot become experts unless we are willing to fail, get up and try again, and again, and again and again.

You are the keeper of your path.

Lay the groundwork for your future and be proud to invest in yourself.

Don't be afraid to ask for help!

Sometimes you learn better when you can do the work yourself. Other times, you should stop being so stubburn and just ask for help!
Hard work doesn't always mean difficult work, sometimes it means smart work.
