One Day or Day One?

My biggest mistake was putting off learning to code.

Start today!

Do as much as you can when you can, and be consistent.


Don't Quit!

It can be overwhelming, exhausting and frankly uncomfortable when you're learning something new.

It can feel like you'll never know enough to make your dreams come true

One thing's for sure; you miss 100% of the shots you don't take.

When you feel overwhelmed, take a break but come back to it tomorrow.

Keep coming back and you'll find yourself doing things you never imagined possible.
