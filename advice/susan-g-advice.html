Hi, my name is Susan and I have some advice for you!

Every error or issue that you encounter is a gold mine.

You will learn so much from troubleshooting and solving problems that arise.

Remember to ask for help!

Don't spend too much time troubleshooting an issue. Sometimes it's a typo that another person could spot right away rather than you spending hours on it and still not seeing it. Also, the validator is your friend.
