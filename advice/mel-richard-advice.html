Hi, my name is Mel and I have some advice for you!

Consistency is key

When learning soemthing new, practice is everything and keeping a schedule is key. I have learned that taking a small, well-deserved break, was much harder to come back from than expected! Since taking that break, I decided to dedicate at least 15 minutes to work on a lesson or a part of a lesson everyday! With summer it is hard to stay inside to learn to code, but a few 15 minutes here and there can make a big difference!

Trust the process!

Learning can be uncomfortable. I have decided to stop looking at job desriptions for a little while since reading them tends to bring a "I can't do that, will I ever be able to do that?" uncertainty in my mind which plays on motivation and "why am I even doing that?". So I choose to trust the process and believe that I am on the right track! I trust that opportunity and growth will come my way!
