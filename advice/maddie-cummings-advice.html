Hi, my name is Maddie and I have some advice for you!

Take it one step at a time

Learning a brand new task can be quite overwhelming. When you feel overwhelmed, take a step back (and a deep breath) and take the task one step at a time!

Work on little bits at a time

If working full time while taking this class, allow yourself to only work on little bits at a time. Don't feel discouraged if you are only able to put in one hour a day. Every little bit adds up! Take detailed notes and refresh your memory regularly throughout the class by glancing over your notes from the previous day before starting a new section.

And finally, just remember...

You've Got This!!
