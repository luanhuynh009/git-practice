Hi friends! I'm Maddie.
Here's some advice for your coding adventures!

Only focus on you and your journey.

It can be tempting to compare yourself to others. If you google tech jobs such as front-end developers, it can seem overwhelming and like you'll never understand what everyone else knows. But truth is, there is just so much out there and so many different ways to code. Everyone is always going to know different things or do things differently.

It's important that you only focus on you and your journey. This will help the anxiety or fear you may have. As long as you are passing and understanding the Skillcrush material, you WILL get there and become a pro in no time!

YOU can do this!

Imposter syndrome is super real when first starting out. I'm still trying to figure it out. Tech and coding is so different - you're pretty much learning a new language!

It's like the foreign language class we were required to take in high school. I remember feeling strange and like I had no idea what was going on. It's all completely natural to feel this way even when learning a coding language.

So try your best to remember your confidence and that you belong in tech. YOU can do this!
