Hi, my name is Nicole and I have some advice for you!

Don't get discouraged!

It can be so easy to feel like you're just never going to get it when first starting out. Trust in the process - it will happen for you with enough practice. Keep a "Growth Mindset." You can truly learn anything with enough time, effort, energy, and practice.

Do a little each day!

Just like learning a second or third language, it's helpful if you do a little bit each day as opposed to doing large chunks but then nothing for days. Make coding a part of your daily routine, and over time you'll get further than you ever even expected.
