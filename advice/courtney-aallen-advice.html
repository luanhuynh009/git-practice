Here is my advice to you as you begin your coding journey:

Trust the process.

It's okay to not know everything when you first start coding and it's expected! It's a new challenge and a new opportunity to grow.

Set an achievable pace for yourself.

Whether you like to dive right in and complete a whole lesson in one sitting or take smaller sections at a time, make sure you are keeping up with a schedule that fits with your life.

Keep cheatsheets handy!

If you are given a cheatsheet, use it! Even the best of the best need a cheatsheet every once in a while.

Don't forget to have fun :)

Learning new things can be frustrating at times. Remember, you are not alone in this journey. Breathe, relax, code, repeat.
