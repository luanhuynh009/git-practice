Hi, my name is Suzanne and I have some advice for you!

Drink enough tea

Make sure you drink enough tea while you're coding. If you drink tea, you get enough liquid which makes it easier to think

Get up for a hoop dance

Put a timer for 27 minutes. After 27 minutes put a timer for three minutes, get up, grab your hoop and have a nice hoop dance. (If you don't have a hoop, do the same without a hoop.)

Moving around is good for three reasons:

  • It will prevent back pain from sitting down too long.
  • It is better for your eyes to regularly look away from the screen. Make sure during those three minutes you don't look at any screens!
  • After moving around it is easier to focus again.

Go for a walk

Go for a walk before your work, during your lunch or after your working day. Or (bonus points!) all three.

It has the same advantages as hoop dance, with the added bonus of some nice fresh air.

Healthy seat

Find a seat that works well for you and that enables you to sit up straight. This prevents back pain. You could try a standing desk, a treadmill desk, a rocking knee chair, a stool, a balance ball, or even better: after each hoop dance pick another way to sit/stand!

Eat cake

Most important: don't forget to eat some cake when you have finished a project! This advice doesn't need any explanation of course.
