Don't be too hard on yourself!

Try not to be too hard on yourself if you're having trouble understanding or applying a certain concept or skill.

Everyone learns at their own pace and learning something like coding can be confusing if you've never worked with it before.

Consistency and Repetition

Being consistent with your lessons can be tough, especailly if you have other responisibilities such as a family or full-time job.

You must push through those tough times and stay consistent, doing so will help build your understanding of various concepts through repetition.
