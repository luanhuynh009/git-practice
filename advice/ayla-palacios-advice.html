Hey, hi. I'm Ayla. Here's my advice to you as you learn to code:

Just keep practicing.

Continuously utilizing your newly learned skills is so important. I've found there is a bit of a use it or lose it thing going on here. If you don't keep practicing everything you've learned, it just kind of disappears.

Be patient.

Don't expect to know how to do everything you want to do all at once. These things take time. Even if you're spending hours and hours working with this information every day, it's really going to take continued use and pracice to get yourself comfortable.
So, keep practicing ;)
