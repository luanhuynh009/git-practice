Hi, my name is Amber and I have some advice for you!

Keep going!

I have picked up and put down learning to code countless times over the past decade. But I kept coming back, kept trying. Trying new resources, new ways of learning, until things clicked. Keep going, even if that means taking a break for a little bit.

Take breaks!

I am a big fan of pomodoros, but often found that 5 minutes wasn't enough of a break to actually do anything. Now, I use animedoro, as taught to me here:

I don't always watch anime, 20 minutes worth of YouTube or 20 minutes of a movie at a time also works!

Find personal projects you want to make, even if you don't know how to yet!

Picking a project you really want to make will help encourage you to keep going more than another static website. I love video games, so I decided to make minesweeper with JavaScript.

Then, add new features and really customize it to make it your own. If you get stuck, google is your best friend.
