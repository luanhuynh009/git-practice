Take it one thing at a time.

When you are learning anything new, whether you are learning to write your first programming language, or how to dance, you go through this uncomfortable phase.

This is a normal part of the programming learning process, and in fact, it's a critical part of any learning process.

Just like you learn a dance move one step at a time and practice the steps you don't know till you get it right!
